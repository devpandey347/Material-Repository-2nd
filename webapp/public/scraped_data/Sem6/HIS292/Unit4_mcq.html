1 Which of the following literary sources is an autobiography written in Chagatai Turkish?

A. Tuzuk-i-Jahangiri
B. Baburnama (Tuzuk-i-Baburi)
C. Akbarnama
D. Humayunnama

Correct Answer: Baburnama (Tuzuk-i-Baburi)

Explanation:

Baburnama is the autobiography of Babur, the founder of the Mughal Empire. It was written in his mother tongue, Chagatai Turkish, and later translated into Persian during Akbar's reign.

Correct Answer: Gulbadan Begum

Explanation:

Gulbadan Begum, the daughter of Babur and half-sister of Humayun, wrote the Humayun-nama at the request of her nephew, Akbar.
