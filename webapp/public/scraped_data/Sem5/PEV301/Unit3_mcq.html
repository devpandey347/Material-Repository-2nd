1 What is the primary purpose of a Curriculum Vitae (CV)?

A. To list every course you have ever taken
B. To market your skills, experience, and achievements to a prospective employer
C. To provide a detailed biography of your entire life
D. To demand a specific salary from a company

Correct Answer: To market your skills, experience, and achievements to a prospective employer

Explanation:

A CV acts as a personal marketing document designed to demonstrate your suitability for a specific role.

Incorrect! Try again.

2 When highlighting key skills in a CV, which type of words are most effective?

A. Passive verbs
B. Action verbs
C. Complex jargon
D. Ambiguous adjectives

Correct Answer: Action verbs

Explanation:

Action verbs (e.g., Led, Created, Managed) convey authority and clearly demonstrate what you achieved.

Incorrect! Try again.

3 Which of the following is the best way to showcase an achievement on a CV?

A. Responsible for sales
B. Increased regional sales by 20% within six months
C. Tried to improve sales figures
D. Worked on a project to increase sales

Correct Answer: Increased regional sales by 20% within six months

Explanation:

This option uses measurable results (quantification) which provides concrete evidence of the achievement.

Incorrect! Try again.
