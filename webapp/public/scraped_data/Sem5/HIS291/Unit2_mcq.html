1 Which of the following materials was primarily used to manufacture the seals found in the Indus Valley Civilization?

A. Limestone
B. Iron
C. Gold
D. Steatite

Correct Answer: Steatite

Explanation:

Most seals discovered in the Indus Valley Civilization were made of steatite (a soft soapstone), although some were made of terracotta, gold, agate, chert, and faience.

Incorrect! Try again.

A. Lothal
B. Kalibangan
C. Harappa
D. Mohenjo-daro

Correct Answer: Mohenjo-daro

Explanation:

The Great Bath is one of the best-known structures among the ruins of the ancient Indus Valley Civilization at Mohenjo-daro in Sindh, Pakistan.
