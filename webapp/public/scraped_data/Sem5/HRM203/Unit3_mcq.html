1 Who is credited with formally introducing the term "Human Resource Development" (HRD) at a conference in the United States in 1969?

A. Elton Mayo
B. Peter Drucker
C. T.V. Rao
D. Len Nadler

Correct Answer: Len Nadler

Explanation:

Len Nadler introduced the term "Human Resource Development" at the American Society for Training and Development conference in 1969.

2 Which of the following best describes the core focus of HRD compared to traditional Personnel Management?

A. Managing compliance and payroll
B. Developing the competencies and culture of the organization
C. Maintenance of employee records
D. Hiring and firing employees based on immediate needs

Correct Answer: Developing the competencies and culture of the organization

Explanation:

HRD focuses on the development of human competencies, organizational culture, and potential, whereas traditional personnel management focuses on maintenance and administration.

A. The reduction in the global workforce

C. The invention of the typewriter
D. Government regulations banning external recruitment

Correct Answer: Rapid technological changes and the need for continuous skill updation
