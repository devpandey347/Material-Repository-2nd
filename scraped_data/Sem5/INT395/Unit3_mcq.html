1 What is the primary motivation behind using Ensemble Methods in machine learning?

A. To eliminate the need for hyperparameter tuning
B. To reduce the number of features in the dataset
C. To combine multiple weak models to improve overall performance and generalization
D. To increase the computational speed of training models

Correct Answer: To combine multiple weak models to improve overall performance and generalization

Explanation:

Ensemble methods combine predictions from multiple base estimators to improve generalizability and robustness over a single estimator.

2 Which of the following statements best explains how Ensemble methods reduce error according to the bias-variance decomposition?

A. Bagging primarily reduces bias, while Boosting primarily reduces variance.
B. They always reduce bias without affecting variance.
C. They always reduce variance without affecting bias.
D. Bagging primarily reduces variance, while Boosting primarily reduces bias.

Correct Answer: Bagging primarily reduces variance, while Boosting primarily reduces bias.

Explanation:

Bagging aggregates high-variance low-bias models (like deep trees) to reduce variance. Boosting aggregates high-bias low-variance models (like stumps) to reduce bias.

A weak learner is a classifier that is only slightly correlated with the true classification (better than random guessing), which can be boosted into a strong learner.

10 What is the main difference between AdaBoost and Gradient Boosting?
